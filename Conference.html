Conferences

With rapid development of information science, especially Interdisciplinary development, it is very important for experts in different fields to discuss with each other around the world.

DFSC organizes and participates in the organization of a series of conferences in information science. Conferences would like to invite well-known experts in theoretical research, implementation and application to give invited topics including: Mathematical theory and its application, Cryptographic theory and its application, Cyberspace security, Artificial intelligence, Digital economics and society, Cloud computing, Secure and privacy-preserving computation, Hardware design and implementation, Software design and implementation, Quantum computation and its application, 5G/6G communication and application, Internet of Things, Autopilot and implementation, Satellite communication and its application, etc..

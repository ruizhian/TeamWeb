Quantum computation and implementation show strong power. How to protect data secure and users' privacy is very important. Lattice-based cryptography (such as Fully Homomorphic Encryption-FHE) gives us the ways to protect data security and users privacy in the quantum world.

QPQC(Quantum and Post Quantum Cryptography) Conference would like to invit well-known researchers all over the world to present their best works about state-of-art, research hotspots and applications on Post Quantum Cryptography and Quantum Computation. Everyone, who does the best works on above fields, will be invited to attend QPQC conference, and present their reasearch works. We would like to provid tranvel funding for the invited speakers.

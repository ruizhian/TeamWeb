    The Secure Multi-party Computation Forum (SMPC) is organized in cooperation with the center for cyberspace security studies of Zhejiang University, focusing on the theoretical research and development of SMPC, the theory of SMPC in the post-quantum era, quantum SMPC and the application of SMPC. In order to exchange and discuss the research results and the application of SMPC, there will be twelve speakers invited to make reports in the conference proceeding.
    All aspects of:
    (1) Theoretical research and development of SMPC
    (2) The Theory of SMPC in the Post-quantum era
    (3) Quantum SMPC
    (4) The Application of SMPC

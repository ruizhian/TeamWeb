Data Security and users privacy is the research hotspots. International Data Secure and Privacy-preserving Computation Conference would like to invite the well-known researchers all over the world to share their research works about data security and privacy-preserving theory, implementation and applications. Everyone, who does the best works on SMPC, will be invited to attend SMPC symposium, and present their works. We would like to provid tranvel funding for the invited speakers.

Invited talks including the following topics: (1) Data security and privacy-preserving theory, implemenation and application; (2) Data security and users privacy in the quantum world.
